Biography

Dayan Wu received the BSc degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2014, and the PhD degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2019. Since July 2019, he has joined Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS) as an associate professor. His research interests include computer vision and multimedia retrieval. He has obtained awards such as the Presidential Excellent Award of CAS, Outstanding Graduates of UCAS and Beijing, Nomination of Excellent Doctoral Thesis of CAS. In 2020, He obtained the first prize for Science and Technology Progress Award of the Ministry of Education. He is also a member of Youth Innovation Promotion Association，CAS.
